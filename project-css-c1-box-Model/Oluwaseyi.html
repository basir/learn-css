A lot of research works have been done in the areas of automatic seizure detection to take care of the limitations (arduous, time consuming, scarce highly skilled neurologists, etc.) of visual analysis of Electroencephalogram (EEG) by neurologists in clinical diagnosis and treatment of epileptic seizures. Methods adopted in the studies include, but limited not to, mimetic technique, template matching algorithm, knowledge based rule technique, neural network technique and parametric approaches.
